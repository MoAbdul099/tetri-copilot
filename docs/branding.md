\# Tetri Copilot — Branding Guide



\## Brand Overview



Tetri Copilot is a modern AI-powered finance and operations copilot designed for SMEs and growing businesses.



Core identity:

\- AI-powered

\- enterprise-ready

\- clean SaaS experience

\- modern fintech-inspired design

\- operational simplicity

\- intelligent automation



Brand positioning:



> AI-powered finance. Simplified.



\---



\# Brand Personality



The Tetri Copilot brand should feel:



\- Modern

\- Intelligent

\- Trustworthy

\- Clean

\- Efficient

\- Professional

\- AI-driven

\- Enterprise-ready



All UI and UX decisions should reinforce these characteristics.

\# Logo Usage Rules



\## Allowed



\- Maintain original proportions

\- Use clean backgrounds

\- Maintain spacing around logo

\- Use approved brand colors only



\## Forbidden



Do not:

\- stretch logo

\- rotate logo

\- recolor logo randomly

\- add heavy shadows

\- apply decorative effects

\- distort spacing

\# Authentication Screens



\## Authentication UI Direction



Authentication screens should:

\- feel modern and minimal

\- feel trustworthy and secure

\- use centered card layouts

\- use clean spacing

\- include Tetri branding

\- maintain strong readability



\## Clerk Usage



For MVP:

\- Clerk prebuilt authentication UI may remain

\- Clerk branding/logo may remain temporarily



However:

\- surrounding layout must follow Tetri branding

\- Tetri logo must appear in auth layout

\- Tetri colors and typography must be applied around Clerk components



\## Authentication Layout Rules



Include:

\- Tetri logo

\- soft background

\- centered auth card

\- minimal distractions

\- professional SaaS appearance



Avoid:

\- cluttered auth pages

\- marketing-heavy auth screens

\- excessive animations
