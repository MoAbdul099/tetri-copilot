# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

This slice introduces the foundational notification framework for the Tetri Copilot platform and provides a complete in-app notification experience for all users.

The objective is to ensure users receive timely visibility of important business events, workflow activities, compliance obligations, approval requests, billing updates, security alerts, and operational changes directly within the application.

This slice establishes the platform notification backbone that will later be extended to support:

- Email notifications
- Push notifications
- SMS notifications
- WhatsApp notifications
- Slack integrations
- Microsoft Teams integrations
- Automated reminders
- Escalation workflows

Only in-app notifications are included in this slice.

---

## 1.2 Business Goals

### Goal 1

Provide a unified notification experience across the platform.

### Goal 2

Improve visibility of approvals, compliance obligations, and operational activities.

### Goal 3

Reduce missed actions and overdue activities.

### Goal 4

Increase user engagement and responsiveness.

### Goal 5

Provide a scalable notification architecture reusable across all current and future modules.

---

# 3. Notification Event Registry

## 3.1 Purpose

All modules shall communicate with the notification platform using standardized notification events.

This registry serves as the official contract between business modules and the notification engine.

---

## 3.2 Authentication Events

### USER_INVITED

Generated when a user invitation is sent.

### USER_ACTIVATED

Generated when a user activates an account.

### PASSWORD_CHANGED

Generated when a password change occurs.

### LOGIN_FROM_NEW_DEVICE

# 4. Notification Categories

## Approval

Approval-related workflow notifications.

Examples:

- Expense approval required
- Expense approved
- Expense rejected

---

## Compliance

Compliance-related activities.

Examples:

- Compliance task assigned
- Filing due
- Filing overdue

---

## Billing

Subscription and payment activities.

Examples:

- Subscription renewed
- Payment failed

---

## Invoice

Accounts receivable events.

Examples:

- Invoice created
- Invoice overdue

---

## Expense

Expense lifecycle events.

Examples:

- Expense submitted
- Reimbursement completed

---

## Security

Authentication and security alerts.

Examples:

- Password changed
- New device login

---

## Workspace

Workspace administration activities.

Examples:

- User invited
- Role changed

---

## System

Platform informational notifications.

Examples:

- New feature released
- Planned maintenance
